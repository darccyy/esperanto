Vowels

Pure Vowels (Monophthongs)

The vowels are the same as Japanese, Spanish, or Toki Pona.

Diphthongs

Diphthongs are formed by a pure vowel, followed by either j or ŭ.

The diphthong oŭ is rare.

LetterIPAEnglish ExampleEsperanto Example
ajai̯byepajlo (straw)
ejei̯bayvejno (vein)

ujui̯gooeytuj (immediately)
